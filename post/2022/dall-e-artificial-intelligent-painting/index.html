Welcome back to another blog about artificial intelligence. In case you are a new reader you should definately read the previous blog too about chatGPT, the AI chatbot! You can read it here.

Where chatGPT is a chatbot, Dall-E is a digital artisan.

By giving it a description of which image you want, Dall-E generates a couple of images for you and I must admit that the results are pretty whopping. Dall-E is also a project that is part of the openAI project and is the first big project that the project released. You can read more, and use it, on the Dall-E website!

I hear you asking, how does it work? Well, it all starts with feeding the AI with data. The more data you enter, the more accurate your result will be. Let’s try to build something ourselves, shall we?
