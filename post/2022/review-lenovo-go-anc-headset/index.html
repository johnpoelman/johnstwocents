Review of the Lenovo GO ANC headset

Dec 24, 2022
3 min read

While the pandemic was ongoing we all switched from working at the office to working at home, todays evolution is more leaning to the hybrid working model. While I like going to the office again everyday a lot of colleagues still work more at home and that means: “Teams meetings”. While at work we use the Jabra headset the comfort isn’t all that well, because of that I got myself this more luxurious and comfortable headset.

So, after using it for a bit it’s time to write a little review about it. When you buy this headset, you’ll find the following items in the box:

  • A durable bag for easy transportation and storing your headset and accessories.
  • A USB A wireless interface dongle.
  • A USB-C charging cable.
  • The headphones.

Nothing special here, the basic accessories every wireless headphone needs. What I like about this is the usage of USB-C. The charging process takes a little more then an hour for a battery that last many hours of usage, more then 24 hours!

For myself, the two most important features this headset has are:

  • Active Noise Cancelling
  • Environmental Noise Cancelling

These features are so valuable when you have to make calls in a noisy office. Everyone is talking, calling, walking by and by enabling these noise cancelling features you don’t get distracted by it. Super!

Controlling the device

The controls are very intuitive, you don’t get many options so for the ease of use this is a plus. You have a couple of buttons on the sides of the headphones to perform various tasks.

Teams button

This buttons let’s you control the Teams application. If you hold it for about 3 seconds you can talk to Cortana. As this is a Microsoft technology I tend to try not to use it. Privacy and stuff.

On the same side you will also find the USB-C connector to charge the headphones, next to the on/off button.

Phone button

The next main button on the device is the button to control your calls. It’s just a button on the other side of the headphones where you can answer or end calls with. Ending a call is done by pressing it a second or two. You can also us this button to control the music you are playing:

  • 1 press: Play of Pause
  • 2 presses: Next song
  • 3 presses: Previous song

Next to this button there are two simple controls to raise or lower the volume.

Accessories

Next to the charging cable and the wireless USB dongle you get a pouch to store everything in. This pouch looks sturdy but time will tell if it really is. One thing I like about this pouch is that every accessory has it’s own place in the bag. Easy to store, nothing to loose and everything fits like a shoe. I’m overall happy with this one.

So, these are my two cents about this headset. Please note that this blog is not sponsored and it is my opinion. Would you buy a headset like this one or are you happy with something simpler? Tell me all about in the comments!
